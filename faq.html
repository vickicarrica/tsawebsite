FAQ

What is the response time?

Our system normally takes 12-48 hours to respond.

How do we know that the doctors are reliable?

Our certification process is very thorough for our volunteers. We do multiple background checks and make sure all of our doctors have the appropriate medical degree. Our goal is to find the best and brightest to address your concerns.

Can I cancel my appointment?

If you need to cancel your appointment please email us at tsamedicomm@gmail.com at least 24 hours in advance. From there we can reschedule your appointment with the doctor.

How do I schedule my appointment?

To schedule an appointment click the schedule button and fill out the form.

How much does this service cost?

Our services are completely free. This is thanks to our amazing doctors who volunteer their time for your service.

What video call service will my appointment be on?

Our virtual appointments are currently held on Zoom.

Can I reschedule my appointment?

Reschedule your appointment by emailing us at  tsamedicomm@gmail.com or calling us at +1(319)633-2666 24 hours prior to your scheduled appointment.
